About

Multimedia artist,
writer, photographer

ECHOx is the working identity of Eko Svenningsson — a Swedish artist based in Berlin, working across fiction, poetry, theater, photography, and music.

The practice centers on a single question: what survives disappearance? Writing names people into permanence. Photography captures what cannot be retaken. Theater enacts what cannot be said.

Analog processes, irreversible techniques. Berlin as both ground and metaphysical space. Language as architecture — syntax and rhythm as structural elements, not ornament.

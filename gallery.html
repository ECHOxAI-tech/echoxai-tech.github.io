On the Practice

ECHOxINSTANT is analog-only instant film photography — Fuji Instax and Polaroid, in-camera techniques, no digital manipulation at any stage.

The irreversibility is not a constraint. It is the point. Each frame exists once, exactly as taken. The chemical process completes itself without permission. What emerges is not corrected, filtered, or reconsidered.

Current projects: Faces and Facades — double-exposure portraits documenting Berlin architecture and residents. The Residue of Duration — long-exposure documentation of Berlin's techno culture, preserving collective presence while maintaining individual anonymity.
